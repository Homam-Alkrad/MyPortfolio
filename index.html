Students Community

Student Communities is a structured platform designed for four main communities: the Student University Community, Student Major Community, Language Community, and News Community. During registration, users simply select their preferred communities, granting them access to relevant content and interactions. Users can also create additional communities or groups, fostering a dynamic environment. Employing advanced machine learning classification techniques Using ML.NET, the platform effectively filters out offensive content and notifies users upon its creation. Built on ASP.NET MVC, SQL Server, jQuery, AJAX, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Bootstrap, StudentCommunities offers a seamless user experience, enabling students to connect and collaborate effortlessly within their academic and social spheres.

Students Library

Students Library, a platform built with ASP.NET MVC, SQL Server, jQuery, AJAX, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Bootstrap. Here, students share books, files, explanations, summaries , pending admin approval.Also Student can share suggest websites in recommend to you section .Admins oversee content quality to enhance user participation. Its user-friendly design ensures effortless access to educational resources, with users receiving notifications upon file acceptance or decline.

Students Competition

Student Competition: Admins have the ability to create competitions stored in the database and announce them at any time and assess teams' answers as part of the evaluation process. Students can form or join teams, with each team comprising a maximum of three members. The student who creates the team automatically becomes the team leader. During the competition, only the team leader can view the answer box, while other members can engage in discussions with the team leader in the dedicated team chat room section. Developed using ASP.NET MVC, SQL Server, jQuery, AJAX, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Bootstrap, this platform fosters collaboration and competition among students in a structured and interactive environment.

Students Chat Room

Within the Student Community platform, students can dynamically generate chat rooms for various subjects, facilitating easy interaction and collaboration among peers. Leveraging ASP.NET MVC, SQL Server, jQuery, AJAX, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Bootstrap,alongside SignalR and Knockout libraries, these chat rooms offer seamless real-time communication. Participants can effortlessly join discussions, exchange ideas, and engage with one another, fostering an enriching learning environment.
